## Motor Driver Bringup
  - 1 Arduino Mega (5V I/O) driving 2 Pololu stepper motors, microstepping on (16x or so)
  - Read serial commands from Zoltaur RPi to start/stop jaw/arm
  - send STEP/DIR output to Pololu stepper motors
  - Arm
    - START/STOP command, stepper spins continuously
  - Jaw
    - HOME/START/STOP command, one limit switch for mouth CLOSED position
    - Once arquino boots, wait for HOME command from RPI to close the mouth.
    - START: move to mouth open -> closed position until STOP/HOME is called.
    - STOP: stop moving mouth
    - HOME: move jaw to closed position hit closed limit switch.
